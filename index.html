Portfolio

Bridge

Winner of the Lighthouse Labs YVR Demo Day People's Choice Award (June 2017), Bridge is a Web Application targeted at couples or friends in long distance relationships, where two or more users can share their own private, virtual rooms where they can write notes to each other, share pictures, or leave links to their favourite content. Users can also videochat with each other while searching for and loading up a YouTube video, and then simultaneously watch that video together during the video chat.

Technology Stack

Rails 5.1 (Server), NodeJS, ReactJS, ActionCable, PostgreSQL, CSS, jQuery

Food Order

A Web App for restaurants wishing to automate the process of taking takeout food orders. Customers are able to select from on online menu, view their order and checkout. Upon placement of their order, an automated call is made to the restaurant letting them know about the order, the restaurant can then specify how long the order will take and have that information sent back to the customer in the form of an SMS.

Technology Stack

Javascript, SASS, ExpressJS, PostgreSQL, jQuery, Knex

Jungle

Jungle E-commerce is an example of how I picked up an unfamiliar language and ran with it. In Jungle customers are able to make purchases and leave reviews about the products. An email reciept will be sent out to the customers with every order.

Technology Stack

Ruby on Rails, Sass, Stripe, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, Rspec

Chatty App

A live chat application where anyone can join and chat with one another. An example of learning a new framework and building on it. Users can chat in real time and the display shows all other online users.

Tweetr

Tweetr is a single-page Twitter clone. Users are able to post messages of up to 140 characters and browse through the list of posts from different users. I incorporated HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery and AJAX on the front-end, and Node, Express and MongoDB on the back-end of this app.

Technology Stack

ExpressJS, CSS, JS, jQuery, MongoDB, AJAX
